# Troubleshooting

## Common Issues and Solutions

### App Won't Start

**Problem:** App crashes or won't open

**Solutions:**
1. Restart your device
2. Clear app cache: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí AI Therapist ‚Üí Clear Cache
3. Reinstall the app
4. Check device storage space
5. Update Android OS if available

### Login Issues

**Problem:** Can't log in

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify email and password
3. Use "Forgot Password" if needed
4. Clear app data and try again
5. Contact support if problem persists

### Chat Not Working

**Problem:** AI chat not responding

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Restart the chat session
3. Clear app cache
4. Update the app
5. Contact support

### Mood Data Not Saving

**Problem:** Mood entries not being saved

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Ensure you're logged in
3. Try logging out and back in
4. Clear app cache
5. Contact support

### Notifications Not Appearing

**Problem:** Not receiving notifications

**Solutions:**
1. Check notification permissions in device settings
2. Verify notification settings in app
3. Check Do Not Disturb mode
4. Restart device
5. Reinstall app if needed

### Slow Performance

**Problem:** App is slow or laggy

**Solutions:**
1. Close other apps
2. Clear app cache
3. Free up device storage
4. Restart device
5. Update app to latest version

### Data Sync Issues

**Problem:** Data not syncing across devices

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Ensure you're logged in
3. Wait a few minutes for sync
4. Log out and back in
5. Contact support

# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### Q: Is AI Therapist a replacement for professional therapy?

**A:** No, AI Therapist is a supportive tool that complements professional therapy. It's not a replacement for licensed mental health professionals. For serious mental health concerns, please consult a qualified therapist or healthcare provider.

### Q: Is my data private and secure?

**A:** Yes, your data is encrypted and stored securely. We follow industry-standard security practices. See the Privacy Policy for details.

### Q: Can I use the app offline?

**A:** Some features work offline, but most features require an internet connection for full functionality. Chat features, data sync, and cloud features need internet access.

### Q: How much does the app cost?

**A:** Please check the app store listing for current pricing information.

### Q: Can I delete my account?

**A:** Yes, you can delete your account in Settings ‚Üí Account Management ‚Üí Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

## Feature-Specific Questions

### Q: How does the AI chat work?

**A:** The AI chat uses advanced natural language processing to provide supportive responses. It's designed to be empathetic and helpful, but remember it's an AI, not a human therapist.

### Q: Can I export my mood data?

**A:** Mood data export features may be available in Settings ‚Üí Data Management. Check your app version for available options.

### Q: How do I join a Community Support Circle?

**A:** Navigate to Support Tools ‚Üí Community Support Circles ‚Üí Discover tab. Browse public circles and tap "Join" on any circle you're interested in.

### Q: Can I change my religious preference?

**A:** Yes, go to Support Tools ‚Üí Religious Support ‚Üí Settings (or tap your current religion) ‚Üí Change Religion.

### Q: How does mood forecasting work?

**A:** Mood forecasting analyzes your historical mood data to identify patterns and predict likely future mood trends. It's based on your past data and patterns.

## Technical Questions

### Q: What Android version do I need?

**A:** AI Therapist requires Android 7.0 (API Level 24) or higher.

### Q: How much storage does the app use?

**A:** Storage usage varies, but typically 50-200 MB depending on cached data and downloaded content.

### Q: Does the app use a lot of data?

**A:** Data usage depends on your activity. Chat sessions and media content use more data. You can monitor usage in Settings ‚Üí System Health.

### Q: Can I use the app on multiple devices?

**A:** Yes, you can log in on multiple devices. Your data will sync across devices when connected to the internet.
